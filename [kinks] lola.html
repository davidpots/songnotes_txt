Intro Riff

Here is the guitar tab used for playing the intro to the song. THough it isn't required, this shows the regular C chord with an added G for the bass note. For the picking pattern at the end of this riff, be sure to listen along to get an idea of the timing. THe key with picking a pattern such as this is keeping your pick hand continuously moving (similar to a strum motion) -- this will ensure the rhythm is even and natural. As always, it is best to listen along to reference.
