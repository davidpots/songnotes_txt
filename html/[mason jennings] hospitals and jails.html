Tune Down 1/2 Step

If you want to play along with the recorded album version of this song, then you'll need to tune your guitar down 1/2 step (which is what Mason does) and use the chords above. Note that this is by no means required to play the song -- you can play it in standard tuning if you wish, but this wouldn't sound right if you tried to play along with the recording.

Guitar Chords Used

Here is how to play the guitar chords used in this song. Very straightforward G, C, Em, and D. Note that most of this song uses the G-Em-C progression, with a very slight deviation occurring only in the "refrain" as labeled above. The Cm, used in the refrain may be a bit tricky if you've never learned bar chords... if this is a problem, you can fall back on a regular C chord.
