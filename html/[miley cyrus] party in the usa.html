A small change in key...

Finally, note that the recorded version of the song is in the key of F#. If you want to play along with that, move everything down one half-step. I much prefer playing it in G, as it allows you to use open chords for the G (instead of F#), Em (instead of D#m), D (instead of C#), etc.
