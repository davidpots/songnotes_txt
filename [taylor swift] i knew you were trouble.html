Capo on 6th Fret

If you want to play along with the recording, add a capo to the 6th fret and use the chords shown above. You can play it without the capo if you like -- but you'd need to play it in the key of F# (in standard tuning) to play along with the record.

Chords Used

Here are the chords you'll need to play this song. The G/B is simply the G chord without the lowest string -- played after the C in the chorus, it helps create a walkdown from the C to G/B to the Am.
