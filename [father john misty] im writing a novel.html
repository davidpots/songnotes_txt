Open Tuning, Capo 1st Fret

These chords / tabs assume you're playing in standard tuning with a capo on the 1st fret. I've seen Father John Misty play this with his guitar tuned down one whole step, without a capo (using chords in the key of G) -- you can do that too, if you wish. A capo is never an absolute requirement (for this, or any song) -- it simply enables you to play along with the recorded version of song without playing chords from a different key.
