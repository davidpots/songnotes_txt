For the Bb-major chord, here are two ways to approach it. Both require barre chord familiarity. If barre chords are new to you, search YouTube for videos on how to practice these (you'll need to build up your hand & finger muscles to be able to play them fluidly).

Recommended Acoustic Cover

Here's an epic cover by Mike Masse, Sterling Cottam, and Jeff Hall. Note, their guitars are (I think) tuned down a whole-step, and they're transposing every chord up one whole-step. So when they play a D-major, you'd need to play a C-major (assuming you're staying in standard tuning).
