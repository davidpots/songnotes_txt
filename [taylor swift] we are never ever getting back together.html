Here are the chords used to play this song... it is essentially the same progression the entire time. Keep your pinky and ring finger in the same exact position for the G, Cadd9, and Em7. Also, note the small variation of going back to a quick D chord after the Em7 in the chorus (and at the end of the pre-chorus).
